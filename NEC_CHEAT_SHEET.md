## ⚡ Topic 1: Electrical & Electronics Engineering
<details>
  <summary><b>Q: What is the total capacitance of two 10µF capacitors in series?</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> $5\mu F$. 
  <br /><i>Formula:</i> $\frac{1}{C_{total}} = \frac{1}{C_1} + \frac{1}{C_2}$. (Note: Capacitors in series behave like resistors in parallel).</blockquote>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>Q: What is the Peak Inverse Voltage (PIV) of a Bridge Rectifier?</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> $V_m$ (Maximum secondary voltage).</blockquote>
</details>

</details>

## 🖥️ Topic 4: Computer Organization & Embedded Systems
<details>
  <summary><b>Q: What is the difference between Write-Through and Write-Back cache?</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> <b>Write-Through:</b> Updates cache and main memory simultaneously. <b>Write-Back:</b> Updates main memory only when the cache block is replaced.</blockquote>
</details>

## 📐 Topic 6: Theory of Computation & Graphics
<details>
  <summary><b>Q: What is the difference between a DFA and an NFA?</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> In DFA, for every state and input, there is exactly one next state. In NFA, there can be zero, one, or multiple next states.</blockquote>
</details>

## 📊 Topic 7: Data Structures, Algorithms, DB & OS
<details>
  <summary><b>Q: What is the worst-case time complexity of Binary Search?</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> $O(\log n)$.</blockquote>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><b>Q: Explain the concept of "Paging" in Operating Systems.</b></summary>
  <blockquote><b>Answer:</b> A memory management scheme that eliminates the need for contiguous allocation of physical memory by dividing memory into fixed-size blocks (pages).</blockquote>
</details>
