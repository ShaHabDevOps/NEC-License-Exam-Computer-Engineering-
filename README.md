## 💡 Exam Day Strategy for Nepal
1. **Pass Mark:** 50/100.
2. **Negative Marking:** None. **Answer every single question.**
3. **Time Management:** 120 minutes for 100 questions. Don't spend more than 1 minute on math.
4. **Ethics Priority:** Topic 10 is the easiest to score full marks. Study the NEC Act 2055.
